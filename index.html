Style & Practical Tips

Look great and feel confident with these expert recommendations

Fashion & Clothing

  • Opt for monochromatic outfits to create a streamlined, lengthening silhouette
  • Choose fitted clothing over baggy styles to avoid being overwhelmed by fabric
  • Wear vertical stripes and patterns to create the illusion of height
  • Select pants with a higher rise to elongate your legs
  • Avoid ankle straps on shoes as they can visually cut your leg line
  • Invest in tailoring - properly fitted clothes make a huge difference
  • V-necks and open collars elongate the neck and torso

Footwear Tips

  • Consider elevator shoes or height-increasing insoles for important occasions
  • Choose shoes in nude or similar tones to your skin for leg-lengthening effect
  • Pointed-toe shoes create a longer line than rounded toes
  • Avoid chunky, heavy shoes that can make you appear bottom-heavy
  • For women, heels are optional but can boost confidence when desired

Home & Daily Life

  • Install pull-down shelving systems in high cabinets
  • Keep a sturdy step stool in commonly used areas like the kitchen
  • Adjust your workspace ergonomically with footrests and proper chair height
  • Use adjustable shower heads for comfort
  • Consider furniture proportions - lower seating can make you feel more comfortable

Confidence Building

  • Maintain excellent posture - stand tall and walk confidently
  • Make eye contact and use confident body language
  • Focus on your strengths and accomplishments, not your height
  • Surround yourself with positive, supportive people
  • Remember that many successful people are shorter in stature
  • Practice self-compassion and challenge negative self-talk
